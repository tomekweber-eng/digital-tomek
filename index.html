About Me

I step in as an interim marketing manager when you need to move fast, looking for temporary support or launching ad hoc campaign or project.

Unlike many consultants, I don't just advise - I co-create strategies, design campaigns, and work side-by-side with your team to deliver visible results, even within tight timelines.

With 20+ years of experience in construction, SaaS, and logistics, I've led initiatives from digital turnarounds for international brands to quick-win short campaigns and projects that generated measurable growth up to long-term brand and communication strategies.

My approach is simple: get in, support, augment or create what matters or is needed, and empower others with tools and processes they can maintain long after I'm gone.
