Interim Marketing Services

As an interim marketing leader, I step into organizations to deliver fast, practical results while setting the foundation for long-term growth. I help companies manage change, optimize processes, and run impactful campaigns — combining corporate experience with creativity and the smart use of AI where it makes the most sense.

Scope of Services

Marketing campaigns

Supporting the planning, execution, and optimization of integrated marketing campaigns — from briefing and creative concepts to execution and reporting.

Rebranding & positioning

Coordinating brand analysis, developing new positioning proposals, aligning stakeholders, and managing the rollout of refreshed branding.

Product marketing

Developing and implementing product messaging and sales tools to support product launches and portfolio renewals.

Loyalty programs and B2B incentive campaigns

Designing loyalty programs and B2B incentive campaigns that strengthen customer and partner relationships and influence short-term sales.

Crisis communication

Developing crisis plans, preparing key communications, and training teams to ensure readiness for rapid and coordinated response.

Thought leadership campaigns

Developing a thought leadership strategy, selecting tools and communication channels, creating original expert content, and supporting brand positioning as an industry leader to increase share of voice.

Marketing automation & AI enablement

Implementing automation tools that reduce manual work up to 40% and increase campaign ROI, including AI integration, team training, and workflow optimization.

Change management in marketing teams

Leading change initiatives: facilitating workshops, clarifying objectives, and supporting team adaptation to new tools and working methods and processes.

Interim Assignment Terms

Collaboration is always based on clear, transparent conditions tailored individualy to each project.

Form of Engagement

Collaboration is possible through a fixed-term employment contract, service contract, or contract for specific work. In some cases, B2B cooperation may also be considered, with details agreed individually. Projects can be carried out fully remotely across the EU and worldwide, or in a hybrid model for clients in Poland.

⸻

Fees and Settlement

All terms, including fees and payment schedules, are individually agreed for each project to ensure full transparency and alignment with business needs. Collaboration can be based on hourly, monthly, or project-based billing, with the option to arrange a retainer for longer assignments.

Confidentiality & Compliance

Confidentiality is ensured through an NDA with every assignment, guaranteeing the protection of sensitive business information. Additional non-compete clauses can be introduced whenever required, providing an extra layer of safety and security.

⸻

Duration

Assignments typically last from 3 to 12 months, offering the flexibility to adapt to short- and medium-term goals. A minimum one-week onboarding period is required to ensure a smooth and effective start. Extensions can be arranged if further support is needed.
